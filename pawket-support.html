Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Pawket
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly within the Pawket app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Pawket app on your device
  2. Tap the Menu icon (☰) in the top navigation
  3. Select "Profile" from the menu options
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile section
  5. Tap the "DELETE ACCOUNT" button (shown in red)
  6. Review the deletion warning and tap "Delete Forever" to confirm
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be immediately and permanently deleted.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes! Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.
